About Scale Turnkey Contractor

Built on one idea: a project should have a single owner.

We were founded to close the gap between designers, contractors and vendors — bringing every discipline required to deliver a finished space under one roof, one schedule, and one accountable team.

Our Story

From single fit-outs to full turnkey delivery

Scale Turnkey Contractor began as a design-and-build studio working on residential interiors. As clients kept asking us to also manage their civil work, MEP, and furniture vendors, we built those capabilities in-house rather than subcontracting them out. Today we run all fourteen disciplines a build requires — from the first design drawing to the final handover — for clients across eight sectors.

We work the same way regardless of project size: one project lead, one schedule, one line of communication.

Why Clients Choose Us

What working with a single turnkey contractor changes

One point of contact

A single project manager oversees your entire project, directing all engineering, design, and finish teams. You get full visibility and a single source of responsibility, eliminating vendor coordination headaches.

Fixed sequence, fixed schedule

Because every trade reports to the same team, structural and MEP works overlap cleanly with finishes, compressing your project timeline.

Consistent quality across trades

Our in-house design, civil, HVAC, and carpentry teams work to the same technical standards under unified site supervision.

Transparent costing

We provide a single, fully itemized quote across all fourteen disciplines, eliminating hidden sub-contractor markups, budget creep, and double billing.

Sector-specific compliance

Whether health care containment, educational safety codes, or hospitality load limits, our teams build to full municipal and regulatory specifications.

Post-handover support

Our accountability continues past handover. We provide comprehensive snags check and rapid maintenance support for your finished space.
